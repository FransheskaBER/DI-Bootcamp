- To create a new environment, fo to VSC go to open new terminal, go to the designated folder you want the environment and then add this to create the env:

py -m venv name_of_env

- hen activate the environment as:

name_of_env/Scripts/activate

name_of_env/Bin/activate



- If you get an error, open the powershel as "RUN AS administrator" and then paste:

Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope CurrentUser -ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned

- Then press Y for yes and then enter
